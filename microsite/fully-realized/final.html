Final Battle

After defeating the Elite Four, the player gets the chance to challenge Cynthia once again in her role as the Pokémon Champion. In this rematch, her team becomes even stronger:

  • Spirittomb: A Ghost/Dark type.
  • Roserade: A Grass/Poison type.
  • Milotic: A Water type.
  • Lucario: A Fighting/Steel type.
  • Togekiss: A Fairy/Flying type.
  • Garchomp: A Dragon/Ground type, now even more powerful

Cynthia's Pokémon are at higher levels and have improved movesets, making the battle even more challenging. To defeat her in the rematch, players need a well-balanced team, strategic moves, and effective use of type advantages.

Each member of the Elite Four and the Champion Cynthia provides a tough challenge, testing the player's skills and strategy. It's crucial to plan your team composition, movesets, and items carefully to overcome these powerful trainers and become the Pokémon Champion of the Sinnoh region.
